Purcell

class project | producer | team of 6 | ongoing | unity engine

Purcell is a serious game currently in development by a group of students at Michigan State University. It is a narrative driven puzzle platformer game designed to help players process their grief in a cozy, relaxing context, for audiences 10 and up.

My Contribution

I serve as the producer and project manager of this project. As a team, we use Confluence and Jira to track our progress and document our design process.
It is my responsibility to:

  • Coordinate meetings
  • Consult with stakeholders and the team to ensure we are reaching our goals for the project
  • Determine the best course of action for the project, then plan sprints and assign tasks accordingly

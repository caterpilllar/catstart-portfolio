Diversity in Games

Diversity in Games is a club dedicated to promoting diversity and advocating for inclusion in the game industry. Started in 2024, I am one of the co-founders and current president of the club. We host discussions on topics in the game industry and how they are related to diversity, such as inclusion in esports, and equitable game studies. We also run events to bring together communities on a social level. As president, I work with the officers to plan, coordinate, and lead events; research topics and available resources to share with the club; and develop and maintaining club principles and policies. You can follow us on instagram @digmsu.


My Work

I founded Diversity in Games in January of 2024. As a co-founder and president of the club I was responsible for stuff and also things.
My favorite event I did with DIG was our Game Jam fundraiser for the Palestinian Children's Relief Fund. Thanks to the efforts of students and community members alike, we were able to raise over $2,000 over the course of a month.
